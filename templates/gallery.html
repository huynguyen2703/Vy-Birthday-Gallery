When She Was Born

Millions of years ago, this world was a wonderland with majestic scenery views, dominated by diverse ecosystems and gigantic creatures. Some were magical; some were unpredictable. During the era when time and space were not restricted due to the development of civilized society. Beyond those measurements of science, a bright, mystical gift had landed, turning the natural deforestation and prolonged drought into beauty; the soil started to fertilize, bare trees transformed into tropical forests, and fruits and flowers began to bloom. Mysteriously, the area where the object chose to descend slowly revealed the object's true form, a reason for all of these phenomena. Surprisingly, an Egg!

The most beautiful egg ever showed up in this ancient realm. Perhaps this was the most unconventional thing that ever happened. From the outside of it, the surroundings noticed the rhythm in each crack of the shell, constantly increasing the curiosity. When the moment came, the shell broke into thousands of pieces of glass, and something seized a chance to escape. February 9th, in the early morning, the land unintentionally woke up to welcome the representative of Aquarius, a little girl with brown eyes.

Naive and Childish

Coming to this world from the sky, legendary narratives were passed down that there would be happiness and motivation wherever she went. Her powerful energy always helps charge up the surroundings; all kinds of animals, mammals, and plants grow under the influence of the little girl. Though possessing such power that could build a world, she still had the soul of a baby, liked eating, drinking sugar drinks, and still learning about life. Because of the age of learning, her inquisitiveness allowed her to pick things up faster than lightning, became proficient in all areas from Science, Technology to Humanness, Reasoning. Over the years, she became more and more reliable to lead this world and ready to conquer the upper levels of growth and self-development.

Beautiful and Growth

The freedom of time and space provided Aquarius opportunities to acquire knowledge and open up about worlds. Since then, she realized that there had been many worlds outside her reach, but her will to try out things she had never tried pushed her off the boundary of her control. Hence, the goal of reaching "new" became possible. Incredibly, the beauty of her soul and appearance are proportional to her potential. From a little kid, she gradually became an exquisite lady, with a stunning look mixed with cuteness, extraordinarily clever, and the original pair of aesthetic brown eyes.

"Birds of a feather flock together". While exploring worlds, she met someone just like her, but there was something unique. That "something" seemed to form a perfect contrary. He is a strong conqueror, complicated, and competitive. His name is on top of all stars, he possesses the spirit of fire, and his symbol is a ram. Undoubtedly, he is an Aries. This destiny encounter of two opposite personalities formed a new combination, such as one of the color theories, "two opposite colors can always go together," or opposite charges attract each other as in classical physics - "electrostatic." Therefore, their relationship started just like a proton and an electron.

Their Love Story

Looking into the future

Together, the couple overcame challenges on their own and shared their journey. Sometimes, they backfired on each other and failed to understand each other because of distinct characteristics. Yet, their alignment in opposition or in another way, once one of them breaks the balance of the scale, things return to the trajectory. No matter how horrible their arguments were, ancient balance rules surprisingly applied and helped them solve their problems. Today is Aquarius's special day. It marks twenty-two years of her journey on this planet, and something is about to happen. Today is also the day to say goodbye to Aries, and she is going back to where she was born, that land of diversity, the beginning of the journey. As she arrived, all creatures and the whole forest prepared to welcome their child. She was happy to see all familiar faces and enjoyed the last moment with luxurious food and treats. At the end of that wonder, February 9th, she took a short walk and bumped into a place where she first touched the soil of this world. Dreaming in this nostalgic moment, she fell asleep. Just before midnight and just before the new day came, a bright light had woken her up. It was full of roses, not just normal roses; they were blue roses in the myth surrounding all around her; at the moment when she was confused between dream and reality, a figure appeared right from the place of the egg; it was Aries. He explained how he could not show up on his love's birthday and had used all his magic to travel to her and seize the moment. Aries told her to let him come next time because they would go into the future together. But how did he know? How did he come?

"Eventually, he showed up instantly because Aquarius's last wish of the day was to have Aries there, holding her in the arms in a forest of blue roses"
